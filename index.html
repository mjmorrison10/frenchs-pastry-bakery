What We Bake

Authentic French Pastries
& Baked Goods

Every item is crafted using traditional French techniques and the finest ingredients. From our ovens to your table, experience the art of French baking at its finest.

Croissants & Viennoiserie

Buttery, flaky, and impossibly light. Our croissants are made with imported French butter and laminated by hand. Pain au chocolat, almond croissants, and more. Worth every calorie.

French Macarons

Delicate shells with chewy centers in an array of flavors and colors. Pistachio, lavender, rose, salted caramel, and seasonal varieties. The perfect bite-sized indulgence.

Artisan Breads

Crusty baguettes, rustic sourdough, walnut bread, and olive fougasse. Our breads are fermented slowly and baked in stone-hearth ovens for that authentic French crust.

Tarts & Pies

Fruit tarts glazed to perfection, lemon tarts with silky curd, and classic French tarte tatin. Each one is a work of art that tastes as incredible as it looks.

Eclairs & Cream Puffs

Light choux pastry filled with velvety cream and topped with glossy ganache. Chocolate eclairs, vanilla cream puffs, and profiteroles that melt in your mouth.

Quiche & Savory

Classic quiche Lorraine, spinach and goat cheese, and seasonal savory tarts. A perfect lunch option paired with a fresh salad. French comfort food at its finest.

Wedding & Custom Cakes

Elegant wedding cakes and custom celebration cakes designed to your specifications. From simple sophistication to grand showstoppers, we create cakes that make memories.

Espresso & Coffee

Premium espresso drinks made with expertly roasted beans. Lattes, cappuccinos, and Americanos that pair perfectly with our pastries. The full French cafe experience.

About Us

25 Years of French
Baking in Orange, CA

French's Pastry Bakery has been the heart of Orange's bakery scene for over 25 years. What began as a small shop with a big dream has grown into a beloved institution where generations of families start their day with a taste of France.

Our commitment to authentic French baking is unwavering. We use imported butter, premium chocolate, and traditional techniques passed down through generations. Every croissant is hand-laminated, every macaron shell is piped with care, and every loaf of bread is slow-fermented for maximum flavor.

When you walk through our doors, you are not just a customer. You are part of our community. We know your name, your favorite pastry, and how you take your coffee. That personal touch is what makes French's Pastry Bakery special.

French Techniques Imported Ingredients 25+ Years

Authentic Croissants

Our croissants take three days to make. The dough is folded and rolled multiple times to create hundreds of flaky layers. This is the traditional French method and there is no shortcut to perfection.

Stone-Hearth Baking

Our breads are baked in stone-hearth ovens that create the perfect crust. Slow fermentation develops complex flavors that commercial bakeries simply cannot replicate. Taste the difference tradition makes.

Community Rooted

We love being part of the Orange community. From donating pastries to local schools to sponsoring community events, we believe a great bakery should give back to the neighborhood that supports it.

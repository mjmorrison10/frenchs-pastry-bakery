The Artisan's Way

Three Days for One Croissant.
We Wouldn't Have It Any Other Way.

In a world of shortcuts, French's Pastry Bakery still does things the hard way — because that's the only way to achieve true French perfection. Our croissant dough is laminated by hand over three days, with 81 distinct layers of butter and dough folded with the patience of a monk. Our breads ferment slowly, sometimes for 48 hours, to develop the complex flavors and open crumb that distinguish artisan baking from everything else.

Our macarons are piped one at a time with the precision of a jeweler. Our tarts are glazed with apricot nappage brushed on while still warm. Our eclairs are filled to order so the choux never softens.

This is not efficiency. This is artistry. And you can taste the difference in every bite.
